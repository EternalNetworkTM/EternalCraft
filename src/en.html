About us

About us

EternalCraft SMP is a Minecraft community, which is part of the EternalNetwork TM gaming community, created to unite, collaborate, and have fun together.

From enthusiastic builders to redstone inventors - with us everyone has the freedom to bring their projects to life regardless of their skills.

Join the vast world of EternalCraft where the sky is the limit of creativity.

Rules

  • By being part of EternalCraft, you must also follow the rules of EternalNetwork TM, which you can find on our Discord server.

  • Don't bully or annoy anyone. Behave rationally and treat others the way you want to be treated.

  • Software that gives you unfair advantage (e.g. X-ray resource packs) is prohibited. Visual mods such as shaders, minimaps and gamma are allowed. Exploiting game bugs is allowed, as long as it doesn't compromise the integrity of the world or affect other players.

  • This includes actions such as griefing, killing players for no reason, intentionally causing lag, or excessive pranks where players can't escape easily or lose their items.

  • Ask for permission and give notice before borrowing anything or using a reserved area.

Violation of the rules is a subject to a warning, temporary or permanent ban depending on the severity of the infraction.

Not knowing the rules is not considered an excuse.
